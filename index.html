How to add it in Outlook

  1. Click Copy signature above for your version.
  2. Classic Outlook: File › Options › Mail › Signatures › New, name it PartsCheck, click in the box, Ctrl+V.
    New Outlook / web: Settings gear › Accounts › Signatures › New signature, Ctrl+V.
  3. Type over the bracketed fields with your name, title and email.
  4. Set PartsCheck as the default for new emails and replies. Save.
